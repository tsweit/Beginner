HTML uses tags to identify contents that will be displayed on a website. The tags also have attributes and these attributes can be informative or functional.

Nesting involves opening an element tag inside another tag. for example the word child and emphasis will be italics because I added the "em" tag to them within the paragraph tag. The "P" tag is the parent and the "em" tag is the child.
